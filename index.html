About Event

Mokola Youth conference is an annual believers’ meeting organized by House of Grace, Mokola Chapter. It is a program designed to furnish constructive knowledge, enable young folks realize their place and authority in Christ Jesus, set men on fire as arrows shot from God’s quiver and help them become a true representation of possibilities available in God as proposed in His word.

As each year rolls by, the Lord has been building us constructively and sequentially, exposing our minds and spirits to "this present truth" in order to be relevant in the present move of God. Its not enough to know and align to what God did yesterday, we must sustain an ability to capture accurately the intent of the Father per season.

This year's theme: “ACTS OF THE APOSTLES” , was born out of the place of prayer. God is launching men, especially young folks into their areas of (not only assignments but also) responsibilities. The revival we seek is here already, but revival only thrives upon the wings of men of stature who are not only anointed but also have the advantage of Kairos upon them. Not every anointing is needed for every season, your anointing must be relevant, needed and configured to serve a particular purpose in a generation. This meeting is not just a conference, it is a roll call or an initiation meeting to assign men their roles and responsibilities in God’s eternal agenda routed through the tool of revival.

Possibilities available to you as you attend
As a participant of this conference, expect the following things to happen to you.

  • Encounter with the Spirit of God.
  • Encounter with the transformational power of the Word.
  • Fresh Fire upon your altar.
  • Miracles, signs and wonders.
  • Exposure to the atmosphere of heaven.

THE PRESIDENCY

ABOUT REV.SOLOMON ADEBARA Rev'd Solomon Adebara was born over 60 years ago in the town of Ifetedo, Osun State. He became born again over 40 years ago in his secondary school at Zaria, Kaduna State. He attended the Polytechnic Ibadan for his A-levels and proceeded to the University of Lagos where he bagged a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Architecture. He founded Adonai Consults Limited, a consortium of construction experts, in the late 90's after resigning as a managing partner at Gideon Associates, an Architectural firm.
He is a minister of the gospel of Jesus Christ and has been in ministry for over 40 years. He is the setman of Fountain of Grace Ministries, Ibadan. He is an international speaker per excellence and has been used mightily by God in various areas particularly in marriages and conflict resolution. He is married to Pastor (Mrs) Titilola Adebara and they are blessed with children and grandchildren.

Our Church

Fountain of Grace Ministries was born on Sunday, 8th March 1992. This was as a result of a divine encounter the visioner – Rev. Solomon Adebara had with the Lord in December 1991 at a place called Ebuebule in Abia State, Nigeria; while on a vacation with his wife and partner Pastor (Mrs.) Titilola Adebara in the company of one of their friends.

Rev. Solomon Adebara’s divine encounter was about the coming of the Lord Jesus Christ with Rom. 13:11-14 as the anchor scripture:
“And that, knowing the time, that now it is high time to awake out of sleep: for now is our salvation nearer than when we believed. The night is far spent, the day is at hand: let us therefore cast off the works of darkness and let us put on the armour of light. Let us walk honestly, as in the day; not in rioting and drunkenness, not in chambering and wantonness, not in strife and envying. But put ye on the Lord Jesus Christ, and make not provision for the flesh, to fulfil the lusts thereof.”

He was so instructed to document the encounter and emphasize in his teachings that Christ coming is nearer than ever before. This formed the basis of his preaching at the inception of this Ministry.
